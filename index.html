Larsen Athletic Scholarship

What is the Larsen Athletic Scholarship?

The Larsen Athletic Scholarship is set up to assist individuals wanting to compete in athletics who are experiencing financial difficulties in meeting the costs associated with training and competition. The scholarship is intended to provide people with the opportunity to participate in athletics at any level and to support them to have the opportunities that participation in sport provides.
